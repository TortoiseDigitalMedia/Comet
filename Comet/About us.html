About Us

Aim


We are paving the way for space travel to become accessible to everyone on Earth, by finding renewable fuels and materials so that we can bring down the cost of space transportation. We are also observing activities in space looking out for incoming asteroid, meteor shower as well as proof that aliens exist.

History


Our team at Comet was founded in Jamaica on May 05, 2017. Four (4) college boys headed out to find investors to invest in their company, they were turn down multiple time due to the fact that they were fresh out of college. No one wanted to take a risk with these boys, but they never gave up and on December 25, that same year they were gifted anonymous donation which kickstarted the company and the rest is history.

Goal


To provide reconnaissance on the Galilean Satellites to compare to those results captured by previous space exhibitions.

  • Our intended financial target: US$75M.
  • Our launch date: August 12, 2021.
  • Our launch target: the Galilean Satellites.
